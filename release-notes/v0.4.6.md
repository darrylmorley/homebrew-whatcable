## What's fixed

- **"Check for Updates…" sometimes did nothing.** A manual click was being silently dropped if the periodic background check was in flight (which happens right after launch and every 6 hours). Now a click during an in-flight silent check upgrades the result to surface a visible alert. Multiple clicks coalesce into one alert.

## Install

Download `WhatCable.zip`, unzip, and drag `WhatCable.app` into `/Applications`. Notarised and stapled, no Gatekeeper warning.

If you have v0.4.0 or later installed, the in-app updater will offer to install this version automatically.

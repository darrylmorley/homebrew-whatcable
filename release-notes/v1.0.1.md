# Menu bar fix and translation updates

A small follow-up to 1.0.

## Fixed

- **Menu bar charging watts now clears when you unplug.** If you had the
  "Show charging watts in the menu bar" option on, the wattage could stay
  on screen after you pulled the charger, showing a figure that no longer
  applied. It now drops within about a second of unplugging. Thanks to the
  Pro user who spotted it and confirmed the details.

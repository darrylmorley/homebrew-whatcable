## What's fixed

- **M1, M2, and M4 Mac mini port matching.** USB devices and hubs on M1/M2 Macs were sometimes showing up on the wrong port, or not at all. Port controller bus indices are now matched correctly across all Apple Silicon generations, including the front USB-C ports on the M4 Mac mini. Thanks to @0x687931.
- **MagSafe charging details now display correctly.** MagSafe connections now show full charging information including wattage, cable identity, and whether the cable or charger is the bottleneck. Thanks to @0x687931.
- **USB-C cable identification via MagSafe CC transport.** The app can now read vendor and product metadata from the CC transport node used by MagSafe, enabling proper cable identification on those ports. Thanks to @0x687931.

## Install

`brew upgrade --cask whatcable`, or download `WhatCable.zip` from this release page and drag the new `.app` into `/Applications`.

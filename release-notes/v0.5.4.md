## What's fixed

- **"Show technical details" setting now persists.** The toggle in Settings was bound to an ephemeral signal, and a regular click on the menu bar icon was unintentionally resetting it (a side effect of how ⌥-click momentarily reveals the technical view). The preference is now saved to UserDefaults and survives across popover opens and app launches. ⌥-click still works as a momentary reveal without overriding your saved choice. Thanks to @wdcs-akshardave for the report (#20).

No other changes since v0.5.3.

## Install

`brew upgrade --cask whatcable`, or download `WhatCable.zip` from this release page and drag the new `.app` into `/Applications`.
